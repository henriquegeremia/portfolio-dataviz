CCR Mapa - Interactive Infrastructure Management

Product Design Project Management UX Research

Project Overview

Designed and managed a web project for public management bodies, featuring geolocated urban mobility projects with interactive filtering tools and visual narratives explaining complex methodologies.

Role & Responsibilities

As Product Designer and Project Manager at Studio Café.art.br, I led the development of this high-fidelity interactive prototype. The project combined my expertise in data visualization and UX to create an intuitive and accessible platform for infrastructure planning.

Key Features & Achievements

  • Interactive geolocation mapping of urban mobility projects
  • Advanced filtering and visualization tools for complex infrastructure data
  • Visual narratives explaining methodologies for non-technical stakeholders
